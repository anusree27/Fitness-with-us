Lats exercise

Dumbells Pullover

  • Grab a dumbbell and hold with both hands. Point your dumbbell at the ceiling.
  • Flex at the shoulder joint while keeping your elbows mostly extended.
  • Flex until you feel a stretch in your lats then bring the dumbbell back to the ceiling.
  • repeat

Difficulty : Intermediate

Lats exercise

Barbell Bent Over Row

  • Grab a barbell with a shoulder width pronated or supinated grip.
  • Bend forward at your hips while maintaining a flat back.
  • Pull the weight toward your upper abdomen.
  • Lower the weight in a controlled manner and repeat.

Difficulty : Intermediate

Inverted Row

  • Lay underneath a fixed horizontal bar and grab the bar with a wide overhand grip.
  • Pull your body straight up to the bar.
  • Return to the starting position, your arms fully extended. Repeat.

Difficulty : Beginner

Dumbbell Row Bilateral

  • Grab both dumbbells and hinge forward at the hips. Make sure you keep a flat back.
  • The closer your torso is to parallel with the ground the longer the range of motion will be at your shoulder. The better the results you'll get from the exercise.
  • Let your arms hang freely, and then pull your elbow joint straight back toward the ceiling..
  • Repeat

Difficulty : Intermediate
