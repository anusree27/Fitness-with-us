Calves exercise

Walking Calf Raises

  • Walk forward and push your toe into the ground
  • Lift you heel off the ground each step and tense your calf muscle
  • Repeat on each leg and walk forward slowly

Difficulty : Beginner

Calves exercise

Tip toe Walking

  • Point your toes into the floor.
  • Slowly walk forwards and backward
  • Keep your heels in the air as long as possible while walking.
  • It may be beneficial to do this last at the end of your workout.

Difficulty : Beginner

Calves exercise

Dumbbell Calf Raise

  • Stand tall with your feet on the ground. You can put the the balls of your feet on top of a plate to extend the range of motion.
  • Imagine you have a string attached to your heels and pull your heels up toward the ceiling.

Difficulty : Beginner

Calves exercise

Barbell Calf Raise

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your knees slightly bent.
  • Shift your weight onto the balls of your feet and raise stay flat footed. Using your calf muscles, mimic a jumping motion but don't leave the floor.
  • Explode onto your tip toes and hold for a second.
  • Bring your heels back to the floor then initiate the next rep.

Difficulty : Advanced
